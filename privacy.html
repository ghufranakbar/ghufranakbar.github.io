Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

This portfolio website is a personal project designed to showcase my professional experience, skills, and projects. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about data practices.

In short: This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no cookies, no tracking, and no analytics currently implemented. Your visit is completely private.

Information Collection

This website currently does not collect any personal information from visitors:

  • No cookies are used
  • No analytics or tracking tools are implemented
  • No forms collect user data
  • No third-party services track your behavior
  • No fingerprinting techniques
  • Complete anonymity guaranteed

Contact Information

When you contact me through email links or external platforms (LinkedIn, GitHub), you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective platforms:

  • Email: Gmail's privacy policy applies when you send an email
  • LinkedIn: LinkedIn's privacy policy governs professional connections
  • GitHub: GitHub's privacy policy applies to repository interactions

Hosting & Technical Details

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses and access logs as part of their hosting service. This data collection is minimal and handled entirely by GitHub.

Technical Stack: Static HTML/CSS/JavaScript hosted on GitHub Pages with HTTPS encryption. No server-side processing or database storage.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of data breach from this website. Security measures in place:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections are secured with TLS/SSL
  • Static Content: No databases or server-side vulnerabilities
  • No User Accounts: No authentication systems means no password breaches
  • Regular Updates: Dependencies kept up-to-date

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there are no data subject rights to exercise (access, deletion, portability, etc.). However, you maintain full control over:

  • Browser Settings: Block JavaScript, use incognito mode, or enable tracking protection
  • External Platforms: Information shared via external links is governed by those platforms
  • Contact Control: You decide what information to share when reaching out

Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to be compliant with major international privacy regulations including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), LGPD (Brazil), and PIPEDA (Canada).

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions about this privacy policy.
